30-04-2026 THE SIGNAL ECONOMY INVERSION — Context for Success.
  Digital 1.0 "dress for success" meant: signal competence through appearance.
  Resume. Clothes. Title. Brand affiliation. Institutional markers.
  A human-readable compatibility signal — but cosmetic. Easy to fake. Hard to verify.
  The question was always: How do I look?
  Digital 2.0 shifts the signal entirely. The question becomes:
    What can I do? Under what constraints? In what context? For whom?
  That is an operational signal, not a cosmetic one. Much harder to fake.
  Old signal economy: dress → CV → title → brand affiliation.
  New signal economy: PUCK → tokens → constraints → availability → trust links.
  Maps cleanly to "Context Before Identity":
    Old model: identity first, capability inferred.
    New model: capability first, identity optional.
  Especially sharp in gig work, micro-contracting, specialist niches, AI-mediated labour.
  Your "work clothes" become structured context. Your competence surface. Not your costume.
  Tagline: Digital 1.0: Dress for success. Digital 2.0: Context for success.
