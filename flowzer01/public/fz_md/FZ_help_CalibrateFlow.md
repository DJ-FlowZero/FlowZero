# Flow Calibration

> **Flow Calibration** is the machine-readable expression of BioLogic — how this entity thinks, communicates, and prefers to work.

18 behavioral parameters stored in `FZ_Flow_Config.json`. Each is a pill-button radio selector with three options. A free-text notes area captures anything the params don't cover.

---

## Parameters include

- Response length and formatting style
- Tone register and challenge level
- Ambiguity handling and scope discipline
- Initiative level, error recovery, and context priority

## In a PUCK

Flow Calibration can be optionally included in any PUCK export as a plain-text block. Any AI reads it cold — no fine-tuning, no platform memory, no configuration required.
