# FlowZero — Product Pitch

> "If I have to explain it, it's easier to do it myself."
> That's the market signal FlowZero addresses.

---

## The Problem

Every collaboration starts with a calibration tax.

Who are you? What do you need? What words mean what? What should I already know?

This cost is paid *every time* — with every new team member, every new AI session, every handover, every brief. It's invisible on a single interaction. It compounds into friction at scale.

---

## The Solution

FlowZero is a portable context-calibration system.

It captures how a person reasons, what they tolerate, what needs stating vs. implying — and makes that machine-readable. Drop it into any session, with any collaborator, and the calibration tax drops to near zero.

**Constraints > Prompts > Model.** Don't prompt better — constrain better.

---

## How It Works

| Layer | What it is | What it does |
|-------|------------|--------------|
| **Profile** | Persistent background context | People, places, objects, environments |
| **Token** | Locally-fixed meaning handle | `[Mitch]` → precise JAR definition |
| **Sticky** | Runtime situational modifier | "What is different right now" |
| **PUCK** | Compiled constraint packet | Pruned, portable, task-relevant |

The core loop: **Gestalt → Prune → PUCK → Prime → Prompt → Loop**

---

## Who It's For

- Knowledge workers who collaborate across teams, tools, and time zones
- Anyone who manages complex, recurring relationships with varied contexts
- Teams using AI who keep re-explaining themselves every session
- Professionals whose expertise is underused because onboarding cost is too high

---

## V1 Use Cases

- **Team briefing**: drop a PUCK into a new AI session — no re-explaining
- **Social planning**: recall practical human parameters (preferences, constraints, fit) for event planning, gift selection, team composition
- **Handover**: pass structured context to a colleague or contractor without a 2-hour meeting
- **Personal ops**: capture household vernacular, recurring decisions, standing constraints

---

## What Makes It Different

- **AI-agnostic**: readable by any model cold — no fine-tuning, no platform lock-in
- **Portable**: a PUCK is a text file — share, version, archive
- **Layered privacy**: Public / Private / Secret — context without boundary is leakage
- **MAP-compliant**: the system is reconstructable from its own artifacts (no black box)
